WHAT NOT TO DO

The integrity of the MEDLOG logo must be respected
at all times. Do not attempt to re-create it, stretch,
condense or otherwise alter or manipulate it.

DO NOT USE VERTICALLY.                                    DO NOT USE A WHITE LOGO ON YELLOW BACKGROUND.  DO NOT DISTORT IT.

DO NOT USE ANY OTHER COLOUR FOR THE LOGO.                 DO NOT USE AN EMBOSSED EFFECT.                 DO NOT USE A 3D VERSION.

DO NOT USE EFFECTS OR IMAGERY WITHIN THE LOGO + TAGLINE.  DO NOT USE ON A CLUTTERED BACKGROUND.

CO-BRANDING                                                                               A
                                                                                                                  2A
On occasion, the MEDLOG logo will need to sit alongside
partner/customer logos on non-MEDLOG applications.
In these instances, the logo will have to abide by the
guidelines of the partner/customer brand.
However, there are some principles that can be followed
to ensure consistency.

When using the MEDLOG logo along with other company
logos, we do not apply the tagline.

When the MEDLOG logo is appearing in conjunction with
other company logos of equal relationship, the MEDLOG
logo should be at least the same size and displayed on an
equally prominent basis. In addition, you should ensure
that the black version of the logo is used for white
backgrounds and the white one for black backgrounds.

Always request an example of the application (screenshot,
draft of the document, etc.) for validation before production/
publication.

  creates a link with the MSC Group. Indeed, MEDLOG
  operates as an independent logistics services provider
  but it is also the logistics arm of MSC Mediterranean
  Shipping Company.
